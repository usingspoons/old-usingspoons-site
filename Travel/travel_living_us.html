About Palo Alto

Palo Alto is a fairly small town about 30 miles south of San Francisco. It is the birthplace of Silicon Valley (Hewlett Packard was started in a garage a couple of blocks from our house) and is home to Stanford University. It was also the first city in the U.S. to have a town webpage ( www.paloaltoonline.com).
