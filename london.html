Things to do in London

We have lived in London for over 6 years. This page lists the current attractions at some of the main tourist destinations.

Museums

Science Museum

Game On - 21 Oct 2006 - 25 Feb 2007
The history, technology and culture of video games

British Museum

The Past from Above: Through the lens of Georg Gerster - 16 Nov 2006 - 11 Feb 2007
Aerial photography captured over 40 years.

Natural History Museum

Shell Wildlife Photographer of the Year - 21 Oct 2006 - 29 Apr 2007
An exhibition revealing the beauty and diversity of the natural world

Art Galleries

National Gallery

Manet to Picasso, a redisplay of modern masters - Until May 2007
A re-hanging of some of the best impressionist works at the National Gallery

Velazquez - 18 October 2006 - 21 January 2007
A major exhibition tracing Velazquez's career

National Portrait Gallery

David Hockney Portraits - 12 October 2006 - 21 January 2007
50 years of David Hockney's portraiture - including many double portraits.

Tate Modern

Carsten Holler - Test Site - Until 9 April 2007
The latest work for the huge Turbine Hall is an "exploration of communal human experience". Or giant helter-skelters.

Hayward Gallery

Royal Academy of Arts

Rodin - 23 Sep 2006 - 1 Jan 2007
A major retrospective of Auguste Rodin's work.
